REQUIREMENTS
============

This project has NO external package dependencies.
All functionality is built on Apple system frameworks only:
  - AVFoundation  (camera capture)
  - Vision        (human body pose detection)
  - Combine       (reactive frame publishing)
  - SwiftUI       (UI)

--- Development Environment ---

macOS:   13.0 (Ventura) or later
Xcode:   15.0 or later
Swift:   5.9 or later (bundled with Xcode 15)

--- Target Device ---

Platform:   iOS 16.0 or later
Device:     iPhone with Neural Engine (A12 Bionic or later recommended)
            iPad is not supported (TARGETED_DEVICE_FAMILY = 1)
Simulator:  NOT supported — camera capture requires a real device

--- Apple Developer Account ---

A free Apple ID is sufficient for personal device testing.
A paid Apple Developer Program membership ($99/yr) is required
for distribution via the App Store or TestFlight.

--- No Package Manager Setup Required ---

There is no Podfile, Package.swift, or Cartfile.
Simply open ClimbingAssistant.xcodeproj and build.
